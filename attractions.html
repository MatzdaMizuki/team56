Waterpark Attractions

Wave Pool

Experience the thrill of the ocean right in the heart of our waterpark with our incredible Wave Pool!

Get ready to ride the waves, feel the rush, and have a splashing good time. Our Wave Pool creates an authentic beach-like atmosphere, complete with rolling waves that will make you feel like you're catching the surf. Whether you're a seasoned swimmer or just looking to cool off, our Wave Pool promises hours of fun for the whole family.

Age and Height Requirements:

Our Wave Pool is designed to accommodate guests of all ages. However, for the safety of our visitors, we have the following age and height recommendations:

  • Children under 12 years old must be accompanied by a responsible adult while enjoying the Wave Pool.
  • There are no specific height requirements, allowing everyone to enjoy the waves and have a fantastic time.

Safety is our utmost priority, and we encourage all guests to follow the lifeguard's instructions and swim within their comfort and skill level.

Hours of Operation:

Join us for a wave-filled adventure during our waterpark's operating hours. The Wave Pool is open from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM, offering ample time for you to ride the waves and make a splash. Please note that operating hours may vary depending on the season, weather conditions, and any special events. Stay updated by checking our website or contacting our waterpark directly for the most accurate information.

Get ready to ride the waves and experience the excitement of our Wave Pool!

Whether you're looking to surf the swells or simply enjoy the refreshing water, our Wave Pool is the perfect destination for aquatic fun and relaxation. Grab your swimsuit and sunscreen, and join us at our waterpark for an unforgettable wave-riding experience!

Lazy River

Welcome to our Waterpark Lazy River where you can Relax and Unwind!

Experience ultimate relaxation as you float along our tranquil Lazy River. Nestled amidst lush greenery, our Lazy River provides a serene oasis for guests of all ages to enjoy. Drift away on a gentle current as you soak up the sun and take in the beautiful surroundings. Whether you want to kick back and relax or engage in some family fun, our Lazy River is the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Age and Height Requirements:

The Lazy River is designed for guests of all ages to enjoy. However, for the safety of our younger visitors, we have the following age and height requirements in place:

  • Children under 6 years of age must be accompanied by a responsible adult at all times while floating along the Lazy River.
  • There are no specific height requirements for the Lazy River, allowing everyone to comfortably participate and enjoy the soothing experience.

Safety is our utmost priority, and we encourage all guests to follow the lifeguard's instructions and swim within their comfort and skill level.

Hours of Operation:

The Lazy River is available during regular waterpark operating hours. Take a leisurely float starting from 10:00 AM until 6:00 PM, immersing yourself in the calming ambiance of the river's gentle currents. Please note that operating hours may vary depending on the season and weather conditions. For the most up-to-date information, please refer to our website or contact our waterpark directly.

We invite you to escape to our Lazy River, a haven of relaxation and tranquility!

Drift away, let your cares melt away, and enjoy a refreshing retreat in the heart of our waterpark.

Water Slides

Get Ready for an Epic Waterslide Adventure!

Get your heart racing and adrenaline pumping on our thrilling waterslide! Brace yourself for an exhilarating ride filled with twists, turns, and breathtaking drops. Our waterslide is a must-try attraction for thrill-seekers of all ages. Experience the rush of water as you speed through the twists and turns, making memories that will last a lifetime. Are you ready to take the plunge?

Age and Height Requirements:

To ensure everyone's safety, we have age and height requirements for our waterslide:

  • Riders must be at least 8 years old to ride the waterslide.
  • Minimum height requirement: Guests must be a minimum of 48 inches (122 centimeters) tall to take on the waterslide's twists and turns.

Safety is our top priority, and these requirements are in place to ensure that riders can safely navigate the waterslide's thrilling features.

Hours of Operation:

Come and conquer our waterslide during our waterpark's operating hours. The waterslide is available from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM, offering ample time for you to experience the excitement and make a splash. Please note that operating hours may vary depending on the season, weather conditions, and any special events. For the most up-to-date information, visit our website or contact our waterpark directly.

Get ready to unleash your inner thrill-seeker on our exciting Waterslide!

Soar through the air, feel the rush of water, and experience the ultimate aquatic adventure. Don't miss out on the excitement - join us at our waterpark and prepare for an unforgettable ride!
