Accessibility




Room of Silence

In today's fast-paced and often noisy world, our Room of Silence offers a welcome sanctuary for guests seeking a moment of peace. This space is equipped with adjustable lighting, offering a soothing ambiance to accommodate different preferences. Soft and cozy seating ensures maximum comfort as you take a pause from the surrounding hustle and bustle. Prioritizing privacy, the Room of Silence remains securely locked when not in use and is made available to guests on a first-come-first-served basis.


Inclusive Access Program

Funpark is devoted to offering equal access for guests with disabilities, striving to accommodate your needs and ensure an enjoyable visit. Our Inclusive Access Program is designed to facilitate comprehensive participation in our parks while considering the safety requisites of our rides and attractions. This program is developed in accordance with manufacturer's guidelines and by assessing the physical and cognitive requirements needed to safely engage with each ride and attraction. Details on all amenities and services for disabled guests are included in our accessibility guide. We promise to deliver an environment that guarantees both enjoyment and safety for all our visitors.


Travel Guide for Autism

The International Board of Credentialing and Continuing Education Standards provides a resource known as Autism Travel. This platform aids parents in understanding their travel options and provides a community for sharing insights, planning trips alongside other families, and discovering travel opportunities at some of the world's most stunning locations.


Offering of Specialty items

At Funpark, we understand and cater to our guests' unique dietary needs. That's why we have a range of allergen-friendly, prepackaged items available for those with special dietary requirements. Whether you're gluten-intolerant, have a nut allergy, or are on a vegan diet, you'll find meals and snacks to suit your needs. For a comprehensive list of these specialty items and where to find them in our park, our meal facility supervisors are always ready to assist and guide you.


Cards with allergen details

At our guest service locations and key dining areas, you will find Cards with Allergen Details. These cards offer essential data similar to what's found on our website, including safety tips, recommendations for dining facilities catering to specific dietary needs, and a chef card for your completion. We encourage you to collect one of these cards upon arrival, ensuring you have all necessary details for a safe and pleasurable stay with us.

Parking Info


We offer ample car parking spaces to accommodate all our visitors. Our car park is well-marked and patrolled for safety. Conveniently located, it provides easy access to the park's entrance.

Motorcycle enthusiasts are welcome, with dedicated moto parking spaces available. These areas are designed for the safe and secure parking of motorcycles. Their location ensures easy access to and from the park.

Our park also supports visitors with trailers, offering spacious, designated parking spots. These areas are specifically designed to accommodate the size and turning radius of trailers. Please note, overnight parking for trailers is not permitted.

Tips & Guidelines



Safety First Approach

When visiting our water theme park, safety is paramount. Please familiarize yourself with our safety regulations and ride instructions. Every ride has specific restrictions and guidelines - for your safety, it's crucial that these are adhered to at all times. Remember, our staff is always present to guide you. Children are bound to have an exciting time at our water theme park, but they must always be under adult supervision. Keep an eye on them, especially when they are in or near water bodies.

Hydration and Sun Protection

With the excitement and thrill, it's easy to forget about the essentials. Staying hydrated is vital. We offer several hydration stations across the park - ensure you take advantage of them. Remember to carry a reusable water bottle to keep yourself refreshed throughout your visit. Additionally, protect your skin. Apply sunscreen liberally throughout the day, and consider wearing a sun hat or protective clothing. Extended sun exposure can lead to sunburn and other skin complications, so reapply sunscreen every two hours or after swimming or sweating.

Mastering Queue Management and Strategic Planning

Navigating crowds and lines at a water park can be made more manageable with clever planning. To beat the crowds, consider visiting either early in the day or staying late, as these tend to be less busy periods. Weekday visits are also advisable, as weekends and holidays typically draw larger numbers of guests. For highly demanded attractions, consider waiting until the morning and noon rushes have subsided. If available, make use of 'fast-pass' or 'skip-the-line' options for popular rides, allowing you more time to explore the park. Plan your meal times to avoid peak dining hours too.

Locker Use

We offer locker facilities for your convenience. Keep your personal belongings secure and avoid losing items on rides. Remember, while water-resistance is common in gadgets, many aren't waterproof. Store gadgets securely to avoid damage. Don't forget to keep a record of your locker number and ensure it's locked before leaving. Please note that the park management will not be held responsible for any loss or damage to your belongings, so it's in your best interest to use these facilities wisely.
